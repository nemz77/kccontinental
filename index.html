ABOUT KCCS

Who We Are

We're a community-owned cafe and bar in the heart of Kings Cross.

Cafe, Bar & Deli

We serve specialty coffee, fresh pastries, and handcrafted sandwiches by day. . .

. . . and by night, we transform into a lively spot offering expertly made cocktails, fine wines, craft beers, and indulgent charcuterie boards.

Ours and Yours

We welcome people (and dogs!) of all walks of life, and strive to create a safe, inclusive, and welcoming space for everyone.
